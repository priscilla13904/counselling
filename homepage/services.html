Counseling Services

1.Academic and Career Mentorship

Academic and career mentorship services provide personalized guidance to help individuals achieve their educational and professional goals. These services include academic advising, goal setting, skill development, resume and cover letter assistance, interview preparation, and job search strategies. By offering tailored support and resources, mentorship programs enhance academic performance, clarify career paths, and increase opportunities for professional growth and job placement.

2.Premarital Counselling

Premarital counseling helps couples prepare for marriage by addressing important topics such as communication, conflict resolution, financial planning, and family dynamics. It provides a safe space to discuss expectations, values, and goals, ensuring both partners are aligned on key aspects of their future together. By fostering a deeper understanding and stronger foundation, premarital counseling aims to reduce the likelihood of future conflicts and enhance marital satisfaction and stability.

3.Personal Issues

Counseling for personal issues provides support and strategies for individuals dealing with challenges such as stress, anxiety, depression, self-esteem issues, and relationship problems. This type of counseling offers a confidential and non-judgmental space to explore personal feelings, identify underlying causes, and develop coping mechanisms. Through therapeutic techniques and guidance, individuals can gain insight, improve emotional well-being, and enhance their overall quality of life.

4.Drug and Substance Abuse

Counseling for drug and substance abuse focuses on helping individuals overcome addiction and develop healthier coping mechanisms. This involves assessing the extent of the substance use, understanding its impact on the individual's life, and creating a tailored treatment plan. Services include individual therapy, group therapy, and support groups, along with education on the effects of substance abuse and strategies for relapse prevention. The goal is to achieve sustained recovery, improve mental and physical health, and restore overall well-being.

5.Mental Health Issues

Counseling for mental health issues provides support and treatment for individuals experiencing conditions such as depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, and PTSD. This service involves a comprehensive assessment, diagnosis, and development of a personalized treatment plan which may include therapy, medication, and lifestyle changes. Therapists offer a safe, confidential space to explore thoughts and feelings, teach coping strategies, and work on building resilience. The aim is to improve mental health, enhance daily functioning, and promote overall well-being.
