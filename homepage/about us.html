At the DeKUT Counseling Center, we extend a warm welcome to all students seeking support for their mental health and well-being. Our center is a safe and compassionate space where you can explore your thoughts, feelings, and concerns in a confidential and supportive environment. The mission of the DeKUT Counseling Center is to promote mental health, resilience, and personal growth among our students. We are dedicated to providing empathetic, non-judgmental support to help you navigate life's challenges and thrive during your academic journey.

OUR OBJECTIVES

  1. To offer Psychological and Spiritual Counselling Services to the students body
  2. To promote the personal or social development of student in a safe environment
  3. To support student improve themselves in all areas and be full functioning individuals
